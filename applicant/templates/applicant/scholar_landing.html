How to Succeed in Your Scholarship Application

  • Fill in all the fields.
  • Submit the full PowerPoint presentation. *Please refer to the template.*
  • Avoid multiple submissions.
  • Applicants should fill out the application as thoroughly and accurately as possible.
  • Incomplete applications won't be considered.
  • Each question on the application is designed to save individually.
  • You can revisit your application as many times as you choose before submitting it.
  • Once your application is submitted, you may not edit it.
  • Applicants may be contacted for additional information if needed.
  • Any omissions or distortions of the truth may result in disqualification.

Ensure you understand the criteria and check your application before submitting.
