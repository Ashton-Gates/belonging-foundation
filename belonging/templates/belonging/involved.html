Scholarship Opportunities



What Are Our Scholarships


The Belonging Foundation provides more than just scholarships, it provides opportunities. We want to make sure the best of all opportunities, get the chance to shine bright!



Why Do We Have Scholarships?


Our vision is to build a more connected community on a national scale that actively seeks to support those who have a spark they need help transforming into a roaring flame.

We're here to find out when one family can endure this much pain in one year, then how much good can we do with all of you in the same time period?



How Do We Choose Our Scholarships?


Our scholarship selection process involves several stages to ensure fairness and transparency:

  1. Application Review: We invite all applicants to complete a template. Our dedicated panel of judges, which includes volunteers and representatives from our organization, reviews all submissions via email.

  2. Feedback and In-Person Pitch Round: Applicants in the top 22% receive valuable feedback. The top 50 finalists are invited for an in-person pitch round at a location to be determined. This step allows us to get to know our applicants better.

  3. Scholarship Awards: Our goal is to offer substantial scholarships, with the top 10 winners receiving the highest awards. The distribution plan is as follows:
    1. Grand Prize: $25,000
    2. Second Place: $15,000
    3. Third Place: $9,000
    4. The remaining $7,000 will be evenly distributed among the other 7 winners.



Who Can Apply?

Self-Identified Black Sheep
This can be anyone of any age or education background. This individual must have a sponsor.

Note: People who do not meet this criteria may still apply with a referral attached by third party.

DISCLAIMER: Scholarship funds will not be distributed to personal bank accounts, peer-to-peer payment, or other individual fund transfer methods unless under special circumstances to be determined by the Foundation. Scholarships are payable to businesses/colleges/professional organizations confirmed by the Foundation's Board.
